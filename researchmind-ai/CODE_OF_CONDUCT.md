# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation to protect the community.
